Status of DTD and stylesheet development

The structure and presentation of the How-To documents are under development. The following documents and the associated DTDs, stylesheets, and sitemap matches, are under constant change. The content of these documents is just sample content and may not even apply to Forrest. Over time, these samples will change so that they are applicable to any project.

We are also conducting an experiment with feedback integration. Go to the bottom of the Multi-page sample to see the revisions which are automatically appended by the sitemap during 'build docs'. Any snippet document with the filename revision-howto-multi-YYYY-MM-DD.xml is appended to the main page.
